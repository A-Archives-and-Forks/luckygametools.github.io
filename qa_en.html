Why can't I access the Steam store after my computer is turned on today

1
Recovery method 1: Use administrator privileges to restart the software again to restore normal use
2
Recovery method 2: Delete the file %windir%\System32\drivers\etc\hosts
Due to computer crashes, power outages, and failure to exit the software properly, the program did not restore Hosts files properly
